About the team

Marcus Russi has worked on a wide variety of interdisciplinary research projects, including leading development of a population-scale simulation model of tuberculosis epidemiology for the Yale School of Public Health, building the stack and data pipeline of COVID-19 modeling platform covidestim, and contributing to the scientific data platform of the MethaneSAT satellite analytics project. He is passionate about the use of software engineering as a tool to gain leverage on complex and important problems. He completed his undergraduate studies in Computer Science at Yale University in 2018.

Sylvan Zheng has built data and machine learning infrastructure at Meta/Facebook, Acronym Columbia University's Brown institute, and is currently a PhD student at NYU Politics and a graduate affiliate of the Center for Social Media and Politics. Sylvan completed his undergraduate studies in Computer Science at Yale University in 2017 and a master's in Interactive Telecommunications at the Tisch School of Art in 2021.
